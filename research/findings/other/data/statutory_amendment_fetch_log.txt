Because the sample had already been drawn from the frozen frame and because changing the ordering after seeing which Act was classifiable would be post hoc, the embedded frame in `statutory_amendment_sample.py` is now treated as the sampling artifact of record. The earlier finding prose saying `63` was wrong.
