# Selected NZ online retailers publish a mixed delivery-fee picture: flat parcel fees, checkout-calculated freight, and loyalty-only free shipping

## Executive answer

- This audit found 22 selected NZ-facing online retailers with materially different public fee models: some publish clear flat parcel prices, some calculate delivery at checkout, some show express options only for selected postcodes, and some provide free shipping only through account, subscription, promo-code, or order-threshold conditions [Kmart FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Mitre 10 delivery and returns](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Bunnings shop online help](https://www.bunnings.co.nz/help-centre/shop-online), [Hallensteins returns FAQ](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns).
- The lowest disclosed standard parcel fees in this sample were around $6-$8 for small parcels, including Kmart urban standard delivery at $6, Farmers small-item delivery at $7, Mitre 10 metro parcel delivery under 25kg at $8, and Briscoes/Rebel Sport standard orders at $8 [Kmart FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Farmers standard delivery](https://www.farmers.co.nz/systeminclude.dialog.readyForShipment.pagelet2-Include), [Mitre 10 delivery and returns](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Briscoes FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/), [Rebel Sport FAQ](https://www.rebelsport.co.nz/faqs/).
- Rural delivery is usually more expensive, slower, or less certain when disclosed: Kmart lists $9 rural standard delivery, Mitre 10 lists $13.50 rural parcel delivery under 25kg, Briscoes and Rebel Sport list $10 rural delivery, Farmers says rural may take longer, and Bunnings says rural Post Haste deliveries are usually handed to the local rural postie [Kmart FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Mitre 10 delivery and returns](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Briscoes FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/), [Rebel Sport FAQ](https://www.rebelsport.co.nz/faqs/), [Farmers terms](https://www.farmers.co.nz/terms-and-conditions), [Bunnings customer message](https://www.bunnings.co.nz/help-centre/a-message-to-our-customers/english).
- "Free shipping" is often qualified: Glassons standard shipping is free over $80 and express over $150, Hallensteins standard shipping is free over $90, Cotton On advertises free delivery on orders $70 and free delivery on a first Perks order over $30, Kathmandu gives free standard delivery to signed-in members but charges non-members $12, and Woolworths' Delivery Saver is a paid subscription with a minimum spend rather than ordinary free delivery [Glassons FAQ](https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns%20), [Hallensteins FAQ](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Cotton On returns page](https://cottonon.com/NZ/information-about-returns.html), [Kathmandu product delivery information](https://www.kathmandu.co.nz/products/blunt-classic-umbrella-green), [Woolworths Delivery Saver](https://www.woolworths.co.nz/deliverysubscription/plans), [Woolworths online shopping terms](https://www.woolworths.co.nz/info/terms-and-conditions/online-shopping-website-and-app).
- NZ Post's public personal parcel rates are not a single baseline: small-parcel own-packaging tickets start at $5.60 Economy and $7.60 Courier, bag-inclusive small-parcel Courier starts at $9.10, large-parcel Courier starts at $8.10, and a $6 rural delivery surcharge applies; that means a $6-$8 retailer metro fee can sit near public small-parcel rates rather than proving a negotiated courier discount [NZ Post sending in NZ](https://www.nzpost.co.nz/personal/sending-in-nz).

**Overall confidence:** Medium - the cited retailer pages are current public pages accessed on 3 July 2026 and support the table, but several retailers calculate freight at checkout or vary fees by store, item size, postcode, marketplace seller, or account status; I did not place live orders or test every postcode.

### Baseline comparison: NZ Post public rates

NZ Post's public personal parcel rates split by product and parcel size: small parcels up to 3kg using own packaging start at $5.60 for Economy and $7.60 for Courier, small bag-inclusive Courier starts at $9.10, large-parcel Courier up to 25kg starts at $8.10 across town and rises by distance and weight, and a $6 rural delivery surcharge applies to items sent to rural addresses [NZ Post sending in NZ](https://www.nzpost.co.nz/personal/sending-in-nz). This is context rather than a direct cost benchmark for retailers, because retailers can use negotiated courier contracts, fulfil from stores, subsidise delivery, or recover freight through product margins; on the public-rate evidence alone, however, a $6-$8 metro retailer parcel fee should be read as a shopper-facing retail price near the low end of NZ Post's public small-parcel range, not as proof of a negotiated courier discount [NZ Post sending in NZ](https://www.nzpost.co.nz/personal/sending-in-nz), [Kmart FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Mitre 10 delivery and returns](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Briscoes FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/).

### Main pattern

The most shopper-useful comparison is not a single "average delivery fee", because several major retailers do not publish one national price and instead calculate delivery after item, seller, store, or address selection [The Warehouse product page](https://www.thewarehouse.co.nz/p/scarehouse-light-up-earrings-assorted/R2925573.html), [Bunnings shop online help](https://www.bunnings.co.nz/help-centre/shop-online), [Harvey Norman Customer Direct](https://www.harveynorman.co.nz/help-and-support/customerdirect.html), [Trade Me shipping options](https://help.trademe.co.nz/hc/en-us/articles/360007263551-Shipping-options). A practical shopper reference should therefore separate "advertised flat fee" retailers from "checkout-calculated" retailers, and should mark account-only or subscription-only free delivery separately from unconditional free delivery [Hallensteins FAQ](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Kathmandu product delivery information](https://www.kathmandu.co.nz/products/blunt-classic-umbrella-green), [Woolworths Delivery Saver](https://www.woolworths.co.nz/deliverysubscription/plans).

## What would change this conclusion

- A reproducible checkout test basket, using the same standard small parcel, bulky parcel, urban Auckland address, rural delivery address, and South Island address across each retailer, would replace several "checkout-calculated" cells with actual prices.
- Retailer policy pages change often; a new fee page, a changed promotion, or an expired free-delivery campaign after 3 July 2026 could change individual rows.
- The Chemist Warehouse free-shipping threshold needs live checkout verification because official pages in this audit showed both over-$99 and over-$50 wording.
- I did not verify account-only prices behind logged-in memberships such as OnePass, Primate, Perks, Out There Rewards, or Delivery Saver beyond public pages.
- I did not contact retailers, couriers, marketplace sellers, or shoppers; a consumer-law or retail-operations specialist should review any public-facing advice that interprets whether a return-fee condition is enforceable.
