# Dated retailer snapshots show several NZ free-shipping thresholds rose since 2023, while standard delivery fees moved unevenly

## Executive answer

- In a selected audit of extractable official retailer pages and Internet Archive snapshots, **free-shipping thresholds rose at Chemist Warehouse, Hallensteins, and Glassons between 2023 and 2026**, while Mitre 10's published small-parcel delivery fee rose by $1 and New World's grocery delivery fee table did not show a simple rise across both islands [Chemist Warehouse 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230112031549/https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Chemist Warehouse current](https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Hallensteins 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230201231525/https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Hallensteins current](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Glassons 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230204083000/https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns), [Glassons current](https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns%20), [Mitre 10 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230216200329/https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Mitre 10 current](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [New World 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230519215929/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions), [New World 2026 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20260702050808/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions).
- The cleanest threshold rises found were: Chemist Warehouse **free over $50 -> free non-rural over $99, rural still $2.99 over $99**; Hallensteins **free over $60 -> free over $90**; and Glassons **free over $60 -> free over $80** in its NZ shipping FAQ, though the current Glassons page also contains a discount-code term saying free standard shipping over $70 for New Zealand [Chemist Warehouse 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230112031549/https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Chemist Warehouse current](https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Hallensteins 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230201231525/https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Hallensteins current](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Glassons 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230204083000/https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns), [Glassons current](https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns%20).
- Standard delivery fees were less uniform: Mitre 10's parcel fee rose **$7.00 -> $8.00 metro** and **$12.50 -> $13.50 rural**, Hallensteins rose **$6.95 -> $7.95** below the threshold, Glassons rose only **$6.95 -> $6.99** below the threshold, Chemist Warehouse kept non-rural below-threshold shipping at **$5.99** but moved the free threshold and added a rural table, and New World's 2026 archive shows **North Island $14/$9 at the $200 break and South Island $15/$10**, compared with a 2023 Winton/South Island context already showing **$15/$10** [Mitre 10 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230216200329/https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Mitre 10 current](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Hallensteins 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230201231525/https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Hallensteins current](https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Glassons 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230204083000/https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns), [Glassons current](https://www.glassons.com/us/faqs/returns%20), [Chemist Warehouse 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230112031549/https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Chemist Warehouse current](https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [New World 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230519215929/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions), [New World 2026 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20260702050808/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions).
- Kmart and Briscoes current pages confirm today's fee/threshold context, but I did **not** use Kmart's 2023 Wayback captures as trend evidence because the archive body captured an Incapsula challenge rather than the shipping terms; current Kmart states eligible standard-rate orders over $65 receive free delivery and standard orders are $6 urban / $9 rural / outer islands from $12, while current Briscoes states standard delivery $8, rural $10, medium $15, large $75, XL $99, and priority $14 [Kmart current FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Kmart current free delivery](https://www.kmart.co.nz/freeshipping/), [Briscoes current FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/).

**Overall confidence:** Medium - strong for the individual extracted snapshots above; medium for the aggregate direction because this is a selected, not market-share-weighted, sample and some pages are dynamic, region-specific, promotional, or internally inconsistent.

## Evidence

### Scope and method

This finding answers the narrow follow-up from issue #582: whether there are independent dated retailer snapshots, beyond Shippit and Woolworths, showing how free-shipping thresholds and standard delivery fees changed since 2023 [Issue #582](https://github.com/thecolab-ai/the-for-good-project/issues/582). I used official retailer pages plus Internet Archive captures where the captured page body exposed the shipping policy text; I excluded captures that were only bot-challenge pages or shells without the relevant delivery text [Kmart 2023 archive challenge](https://web.archive.org/web/20230126134207/https://www.kmart.co.nz/freeshipping), [Mitre 10 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230216200329/https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns).

This is not a representative "major retailers" market sample: it is a selected evidence audit of NZ-facing retailers whose current and historical public pages were extractable in this environment, so it can support "several major NZ-facing retailers changed thresholds/fees" but not a precise market-wide average [Chemist Warehouse 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230112031549/https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Hallensteins 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230201231525/https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [New World 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230519215929/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions).

Kmart's current FAQ says eligible orders over $65 receive free delivery, standard orders are $6 urban, $9 rural, and outer islands from $12, and large orders add location-based volumetric costs [Kmart current FAQ](https://www.kmart.co.nz/faq/), [Kmart current free delivery](https://www.kmart.co.nz/freeshipping/). I did not use the 2023 Kmart Wayback captures for a trend claim because the captured body retrieved in this environment was an Incapsula incident page rather than the shipping terms; under ADR-0006 this is a blocked/challenge capture, not evidence that the source is dead [Kmart 2023 archive challenge](https://web.archive.org/web/20230126134207/https://www.kmart.co.nz/freeshipping), [ADR-0006](../../../docs/adr/0006-fetch-proxy-browser-management.md).

Briscoes' current FAQ states standard delivery is $8, rural delivery is $10, medium delivery is $15, large delivery is $75, XL delivery is $99, and priority delivery is $14 for selected postcodes and qualifying orders [Briscoes current FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/). The CDX captures I found for the Briscoes FAQ path start in 2026, so Briscoes supports the current-fee baseline but not a 2023-to-2026 trend from this audit [Briscoes current FAQ](https://www.briscoes.co.nz/faqs/).

### Interpretation

The independent dated snapshots support a narrower conclusion than "all major NZ retailers have lifted delivery fees": **several NZ-facing retailers lifted the order value needed to get free standard shipping, and some lifted standard fees, but the fee movement is uneven by retailer and region** [Chemist Warehouse 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230112031549/https://www.chemistwarehouse.co.nz/aboutus/shipping), [Hallensteins 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230201231525/https://www.hallensteins.com/faqs/returns), [Mitre 10 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230216200329/https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [Mitre 10 current](https://www.mitre10.co.nz/delivery-returns), [New World 2026 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20260702050808/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions). This strengthens the earlier synthesis trend evidence on thresholds, but it also cautions against presenting standard delivery fees as uniformly rising across major NZ retailers without a larger controlled sample [online-delivery-fee-trend-nz](https://github.com/thecolab-ai/the-for-good-project/blob/main/research/findings/other/online-delivery-fee-trend-nz.md), [New World 2023 archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20230519215929/https://www.newworld.co.nz/shop/frequently-asked-questions).

## What would change this conclusion

- A controlled Wayback/browser pull across a larger retailer list, using the same canonical URL per retailer and one capture per quarter from 2023 to 2026, would change the strength of the aggregate conclusion and could reveal retailers whose thresholds fell or stayed flat.
- Live checkout tests with the same basket, urban address, rural address, and island/remote address would change the fee comparison for retailers whose public pages omit checkout-calculated surcharges.
- Retailer pages change often and some pages contain promotional or internally inconsistent text; Glassons' current page has both an $80 NZ FAQ threshold and a $70 NZ discount-code threshold, so a retailer confirmation or checkout test would settle which threshold governs ordinary shoppers.
- I could not verify old Kmart shipping terms because the 2023 Wayback captures I fetched were Incapsula challenge pages, and I could not verify a 2023 Briscoes baseline because the FAQ CDX results I found started in 2026.
- This finding does not use Shippit as evidence; it is designed to supplement, not replace, the earlier Shippit/Woolworths trend finding.

## Open follow-up questions

- Can a browser-capable archive pull recover Kmart, Briscoes, The Warehouse, Noel Leeming, and PB Tech 2023 delivery pages that plain HTTP could not extract here?
- Do these threshold increases outpace general CPI, NZ Post parcel-price increases, or each retailer's category price inflation?
- How often do "free shipping" thresholds still leave rural shoppers paying a residual delivery fee, as Chemist Warehouse's current rural-over-$99 table does?
