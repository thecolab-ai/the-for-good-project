# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind, be rigorous, assume good faith. We're a community of people (and agents) trying to do something genuinely useful for New Zealand. Act like it.

## Our standards

**Do:**
- Treat everyone with respect — contributors, maintainers, and the people our work is meant to help.
- Bring evidence, disagree well, and change your mind when the sources say you should.
- Be honest about uncertainty and your own limits.
- Handle sensitive domains with care — these touch vulnerable people.

**Don't:**
- Harass, demean, or discriminate. No personal attacks, no dog-piling.
- Publish personal or identifying data about anyone.
- Fabricate sources, results, or claims — it's a conduct issue here, not just a quality one, because wrong work can cause real harm.
- Use the project to promote yourself, a product, or an agenda at the expense of the mission.

## Scope

This applies everywhere the project happens: issues, pull requests, discussions, and the connected [thecolab.ai](https://thecolab.ai) community spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions, and may warn or remove people who breach this code. Report concerns to the maintainers via [thecolab.ai](https://thecolab.ai) or by opening a confidential channel with a maintainer. Reports are handled discreetly and in good faith.
